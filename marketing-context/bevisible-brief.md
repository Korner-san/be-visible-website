Executive Summary

In a world where decisions are no longer made through search results but through AI-generated answers, visibility has fundamentally changed.

Be-Visible exists to ensure that when AI models speak —
your brand is part of the answer.

What the Service Delivers
Authoritative Presence Inside AI Systems

Be-Visible enables brands to systematically influence how they are represented inside leading AI models such as ChatGPT and Perplexity AI.

Instead of competing for clicks, clients gain control over:

where they appear
how often they are mentioned
and how they are positioned against competitors

This is not traditional SEO.
This is ownership of narrative inside AI-generated intelligence.

A Proprietary Intelligence Layer Built Around Your Brand

At the core of the service is a continuously evolving intelligence system built specifically around each client’s market.

Be-Visible constructs a high-fidelity data layer by:

systematically querying AI environments with the most commercially relevant prompts
capturing responses, citations, and entity relationships
analyzing every referenced source at scale

The result is not raw data —
but a refined, structured understanding of how AI models perceive your category, your competitors, and your brand.

From Intelligence to Execution — Seamlessly Integrated

Be-Visible goes beyond delivering insight.
It establishes a direct operational bridge between intelligence and action.

In an environment increasingly driven by autonomous systems, brands are no longer executing strategies manually —
they are deploying agents to interpret, decide, and act.

Be-Visible is designed for this reality.

Clients are equipped not only with a deeply structured data foundation, but with the ability to connect that foundation directly into their internal systems and agent workflows.

Through tailored integrations, including custom API architectures, Be-Visible enables:

seamless access to a continuously evolving intelligence layer
structured delivery of insights in a format optimized for automated decision-making
alignment between strategic data and execution systems

This transforms Be-Visible from a source of insight into a core infrastructure layer —
one that empowers both human teams and autonomous agents to operate with precision, clarity, and speed.

Precision Mapping of Competitive Positioning

Clients receive a clear, distilled view of:

which competitors are consistently surfaced by AI models
why they are favored
and where strategic gaps exist

This eliminates guesswork and replaces it with direct, evidence-based positioning intelligence —
allowing brands to act with certainty rather than assumption.

Clarity on What Drives AI Visibility

Be-Visible identifies the exact content patterns and signals that influence AI-generated answers.

Clients gain:

a precise understanding of which content formats dominate their category
clear direction for both on-site and off-site strategy
actionable insights grounded in observed AI behavior — not theory

This transforms marketing execution into a targeted, outcome-driven process.

Continuous Discovery of High-Value Opportunities

The system continuously surfaces the most relevant queries shaping your market, ensuring alignment with real demand.

Rather than relying on static keyword research, clients operate with a living map of high-impact questions —
the exact moments where AI models decide which brands to include.

Strategic Guidance at Executive Level

Beyond the platform, clients receive ongoing collaboration with a dedicated expert team.

Through structured, recurring sessions, Be-Visible provides:

interpretation of data and trends
prioritization of actions
alignment between insight and execution

This ensures that insights are not only understood —
but translated into measurable advantage.

The Outcome

Be-Visible does not offer visibility in the traditional sense.

It provides something far more valuable:

Control over how your brand is represented inside the systems that increasingly define consumer decisions — and the ability to act on that intelligence at scale.

As AI becomes the primary interface between users and information,
brands that are not present in these answers become invisible.

Be-Visible ensures that does not happen.

Positioning Statement

Be-Visible is not a tool.
It is not a dashboard.
It is not another marketing channel.

It is a strategic intelligence layer —
and an execution bridge into the emerging ecosystem of AI-driven decision systems.

A foundation designed for a new reality,
where AI decides what the market sees, trusts, and chooses.

## Additional Positioning Decisions

### Primary audience

The primary audience for BeVisible is business and marketing decision-makers.

This includes:
- CEOs
- Founders
- CMOs
- VPs of Marketing
- Heads of Growth
- Brand managers
- PR leaders

The landing page copy should speak mainly to business and marketing leaders, not technical teams.

The copy should focus on:
- brand visibility
- competitive positioning
- market perception
- customer discovery through AI-generated answers
- the risk of becoming invisible inside AI answers

Technical features such as APIs, integrations, data infrastructure, and agent workflows can be mentioned later as supporting capabilities, but they should not lead the main website message.

### Proof points

There are currently no public client logos, testimonials, case studies, or verified performance metrics that should be used on the website.

Internal testing and pilot data exist, and the system works, but this should not be presented as public proof at this stage.

Do not invent:
- client names
- numbers
- results
- case studies
- testimonials
- measurable improvement claims

The website copy should rely on clear positioning, concrete product capabilities, and the urgency of the market shift rather than fake social proof.

### Current alternative / enemy

The main alternative is inaction.

Most brands are not actively solving this problem because they do not yet understand that AI-generated answers are becoming a new discovery layer for customers.

Even when brands do understand that the problem exists, they often do not know:
- what to measure
- which prompts matter
- which AI models to track
- why competitors are being recommended
- what content or authority signals influence those answers
- what actions they should take next
- which systems they need in order to solve the problem

The real enemy is not a specific tool.

The real enemy is uncertainty, lack of visibility, and lack of a clear operational path inside AI-generated discovery.
